Neuromancer Archive Protocol

> Neuromancer is an experimental music circle and digital repository dedicated to the intersection of brutalist architecture and modular synthesis. We exist in the silences between data packets, curating soundscapes that reflect the quiet authority of high-end technical documentation and early-internet archives.

Our philosophy is rooted in Precision, Silence, and Preservation. We reject the clutter of modern distribution in favor of a "Windowed Framing" approach—where every release is treated as a specimen, every track as a metadata entry.

The Digital Curator does not merely broadcast; we archive. We specialize in high-fidelity sonic artifacts that evoke the cold, focused atmosphere of a server room at midnight. No neon glows, no glitch clichés—only the steady, intentional pulse of the machine.
